# Privacy Policy — EverTemp

*Last updated: April 26, 2025*

---

## Summary

EverTemp does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including temperature readings, symptoms, medication records, and profile details — is stored **exclusively on your device** and never leaves it.

---

## Data We Collect

**None.**

EverTemp does not collect any personal data, usage statistics, analytics, crash reports, or any other information from your device.

## Local Storage

All data you enter in EverTemp (temperature logs, profiles, notes, medication records) is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device. This data is:

- Never uploaded to any server
- Never shared with third parties
- Never used for advertising or analytics
- Accessible only through the EverTemp app on your device

## Notifications

If you enable dose reminders, EverTemp schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any external server or service.

## AI Consult Feature

The AI consult feature copies a plain-text summary of your recent temperature and medication records to your **clipboard**. This text is not transmitted by EverTemp — you choose whether and where to paste it. EverTemp has no control over third-party apps you paste into.

## Children's Privacy

EverTemp does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
